# IBM style with the "Paul Rand" heritage look

## Prompt Text

A professional presentation slide in the classic 1960s IBM graphic design style by Paul Rand. Features a strict Swiss grid layout, bold primary colors (IBM Blue, black, and white), and iconic striped motifs. Minimalist flat vector illustrations. High use of negative space. The aesthetic is mid-century modern, sophisticated, and corporate. High-fidelity rendering with a matte paper texture.
Best for: Creative, iconic, and legendary "Think" vibes. Focuses on bold motifs and the 8-bar logo aesthetic. Please DO NOT add "IBM" logo on the slides.
